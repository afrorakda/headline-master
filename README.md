# headline-master

Part of the Fridge Combo project. Automatically format your headlines into professional Title Case.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action

Just paste your title. Headline Master intelligently capitalizes major words while keeping minor words (like "of," "the," or "with") lowercase, following professional AP Style guidelines.

## Smart & Professional

Perfect for bloggers, developers, and creators writing READMEs, social posts, or article titles. Make your English headlines look sharp and authoritative in one click.
